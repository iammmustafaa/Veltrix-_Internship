Font Pairing:
Font pairing means selecting two or more fonts that work well together in one design. A good combination creates balance and improves the overall appearance. Usually, designers pair a bold font for headings with a simple and clean font for body text. The fonts should be different enough to create contrast but still look harmonious together. Proper font pairing makes a design more attractive and organized.
Readability vs Creativity:
Readability means how easy the text is to read and understand. Creativity means making the text look unique and visually interesting. In design, both are important, but readability should always come first. If a font looks stylish but is difficult to read, the message may be lost. A good designer balances creativity with clarity to make designs both eye-catching and effective.
